Pizza Quattro Formaggi

Quattro Formaggi Pizza is a traditional Italian pizza dating back to the 18th century that is topped with four different cheeses. Italians make this pizza either rossa (with tomato paste) or bianca (without tomato).

As you’ve probably guessed, the name is fairly self-explanatory. Indeed, in Italian, quattro means four and formaggi means cheeses. But, what is less obvious is which cheeses should you use?.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups plain Tipo 00 flour
  • 2 tsp fast action dried yeast
  • 320 ml lukewarm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • semolina flour for dusting
